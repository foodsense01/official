Nurturing Healthier Lifestyles, One Plate at a Time

FoodSense is a non-profit organization committed to nurturing healthier lifestyles by encouraging small, sustainable changes in everyday eating habits. Guided by the belief that wellness begins with the food we consume, FoodSense empowers individuals to make informed dietary choices—like swapping out processed foods for fresh, whole ingredients, increasing their intake of fruits and vegetables, and practicing mindful eating. The organization’s mission is rooted in accessibility and simplicity, making healthy living achievable for everyone, regardless of age or background. As part of its outreach, FoodSense actively conducts seminars and interactive workshops in schools to educate young minds about nutrition, the importance of balanced meals, and the long-term benefits of healthy habits. Alongside these efforts, it offers a range of free resources including culturally rooted healthy Indian recipes, motivational content, and practical wellness tips. Through community engagement and education, FoodSense is creating a ripple effect of positive change—one plate, one person, and one school at a time.

Healthy Indian Recipes

Chickpea Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1 small cucumber, diced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Mix all ingredients in a bowl, add lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Serve fresh.

Vegetable Pulao

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 green chilies
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

Wash rice and cook with vegetables and spices until fluffy. Serve hot.

Fruit Chaat

Ingredients:

  • Chopped apples, bananas, pomegranate, mango
  • Chaat masala
  • Black salt
  • Lemon juice

Instructions:

Mix all the fruits in a bowl, sprinkle chaat masala, black salt, and lemon juice. Toss and serve.
